The Era of Video Games

As the rise of computers advanced, so did video games. From 2D sprites to 3D, fully rendered worlds, and from 8-bit graphics to 4k realism. Video games have shown significant change in the past 30 years. To say it as a single topic would be a disservice to the gaming industry, as so many parts of video games of today have advanced the industry altogether.

Video games stand on the shoulders of giants, as they are the culmination of a lot of hard work and ingenuity. Instead of reading a book and wondering what the world would be like, you can play in that world with video games. Same applies for movies, but your actions are your own, not a scripted one (albeit some games are linearly crafted.)
